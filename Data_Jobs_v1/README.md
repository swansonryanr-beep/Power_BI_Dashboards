# Data Jobs Dashboard w/ Power BI

![Dashboard Page 1](/images/power_bi_page_1.png)

## Introduction

This dashboard was created for job seekers, career transitioners, and professionals exploring opportunities in the data industry. Using a real-world dataset of 2024 data job postings, the report consolidates salary, location, and hiring trends into a single interactive dashboard, making it easier to explore the current job market and identify career opportunities.

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- Power Query
- Data Transformation (ETL)
- Data Visualization
- Interactive Dashboards
- Drill-Through Reporting
- Bookmarks & Navigation
- Geospatial Analysis

## Skills Showcased

- **Data Transformation (ETL) with Power Query:** 
Cleaned, shaped, and prepared the raw data for analysis by handling blanks, changing data types, and creating new columns. 

- **Aggregations & KPIs:** Leveraged Power BI's built-in aggregation capabilities to calculate key metrics such as Median Yearly Salary, Median Hourly Salary, and Job Count.

- **Core Charts:** Utilized **Column, Bar, Line, and Area Charts** to compare job counts, salary trends, and market demand across roles.

- **Geospatial Analysis:** Leveraged Map Charts to visualize the global distribution of data jobs and identify geographic hiring trends.

- **KPI Indicators & Tables:** Used **Cards** to display key metrics and **Tables** to provide granular, sortable data. 

- **Dashboard Design:** Designed an intuitive and visually appealing layout, exploring both common and less frequently used visualizations to effectively communicate insights.

- **Interactive Reporting:** 
    - **Slicers:**
    Implemented slicers to dynamically filter the report by Job Title.

    - **Buttons & Bookmarks:** To create a seamless navigation experience between report pages and dashboard views.

    - **Drill-Through:** Implemented drill-through functionality to navigate from high-level summaries to detailed contextual analysis.

## Dashboard Overview

### Page 1: High-Level Market View

![Dashboard Page 1](/images/power_bi_page_1.png)

This is your mission control for the data job market. It showcases key KPIs like total job count, median salaries, and top job titles to give you a quick understanding of what's happening in the job market at a glance. 

### Page 2: Job Title Drill Through

![Dashboard Page 2](/images/power_bi_page_2.png)

This is the deep-dive page. From the main dashboard, you can drill through this view to get specific details for a single job title, including salary ranges, work-from-home stats, top hiring platforms, and a global map of job locations. 

## Key Insights

Through analysis of the 2024 data jobs market, several trends emerged:

- Data-related roles continue to offer strong compensation across industries.
- Salary ranges vary significantly depending on job title and specialization.
- Remote opportunities remain a meaningful portion of the market.
- Geographic location continues to influence both job availability and compensation.
- Certain job titles consistently appear among the highest-demand positions.
