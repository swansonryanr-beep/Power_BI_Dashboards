![Dashboard Page 1](/images/power_bi_proj_2.png)

## Introduction

This dashboard was created for job seekers, career transitioners, and professionals exploring opportunities in the data industry. Using a real-world dataset of 2024 data job postings, the dashboard consolidates salary, location, and hiring trends, all within a single-page, streamlined, interactive interface to quickly explore different roles, market trends, and compensation insights.

## Business Questions Answered

This dashboard was designed to help answer questions such as:

- Which data roles offer the highest compensation?
- What technical skills appear most frequently in job postings?
- How do salaries vary across data-related careers?
- What hiring trends emerged throughout 2024?
- How does compensation differ by country and role?

## Key Insights

Analysis of the 2024 data jobs market revealed several notable trends:

- Python and SQL consistently appeared among the most requested technical skills across data-related roles.
- Senior Data Scientist, Machine Learning Engineer, and Senior Data Engineer roles offered some of the highest median compensation levels.
- Data Analyst and Data Engineer positions represented a significant portion of overall job demand.
- Salary expectations varied considerably across job titles, highlighting the importance of specialization within the data field.
- Hiring demand fluctuated throughout the year, with certain months showing noticeably higher posting activity than others.
- Geographic filtering revealed differences in compensation and job availability across countries and regions.
- The average job posting required approximately 4.8 skills, demonstrating the broad technical expectations placed on data professionals.
